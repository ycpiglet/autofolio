## Insight

1. The manual bank-transfer workflow is now testable without only relying on a human visual search through requests.
2. Recognition remains advisory; the Owner still makes the explicit activation decision and creates the member account.
3. Production payment recognition still needs a separate decision for audit storage, provider/API choice, and multi-tenant isolation.

## Decision

1. Keep pasted bank statement text stateless and do not store raw payment records in the repo or local vault.
2. Use `code_assisted_deposit_match` only when the Owner confirms a high-confidence match.
3. Treat real bank API/open-banking/PG and production payment records as Owner-gated TASK-087 follow-up.

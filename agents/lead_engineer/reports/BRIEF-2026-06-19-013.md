## Insight

1. The local membership flow no longer treats approved members as implicit Owners.
2. Members can still complete required self-service setup without touching global trading controls.
3. This is a stopgap before true production multi-tenant data isolation, not a replacement for Supabase/RLS design.

## Decision

1. Keep Owner/admin APIs owner-only until user_id isolation is implemented.
2. Allow only session-derived self-service mutation for member accounts.
3. Treat read-scope, guest/demo policy, Supabase schema, and per-user engine isolation as TASK-087 follow-up.
